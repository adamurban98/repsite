Academic representatives

Coursework, labwork, assesment, lecturers. If you have anything regarding these talk to them.

Well-being representatives

Coffee room, workplace in the department, common room for UGs? The well being reps are here to make our studies more comfortable by overlooking these facilities.

Dep-reps

Departmental representatives. They represent all 4 years of EIE altogether, we can contact them if a more serious issue arises.

Proposal submissions form

This is a form where you can submit anything that you would like us to deal with. We are here to help you but we will also welcome constructive remarks.

Confidentiality

You can chose wheter to deal with your issue privately or publicly. We will take confidentiality very seriously. The content of the submission form is not sent to the staff directly. Instead it serves as a tool for us to know which issues to raise during the Staff-Student-Committee meetings.

Staff-Student-Committee meeting

This is a regular meeting which gives us a great opportunity to present our view on the development of the EE department.
